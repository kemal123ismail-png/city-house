Our Story

A Gem in the Heart of Bitola

Nestled in the historic center of Bitola — Macedonia's most elegant city — City House offers a sanctuary where old-world charm meets contemporary luxury. Our boutique hotel combines modern design with genuine Macedonian warmth.

Every room is a carefully curated space: spotlessly clean, flooded with natural light, and equipped with everything you need. Our hosts Igor and Anica go above and beyond to make every guest feel truly at home.
